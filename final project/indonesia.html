Indonesia

Indonesian coffees take to dark roasting well, and so there are often smoky and toasted flavors present in the cup. Flavors can range from stouty and woodsy with a complexity that is both savory and herbaceous, to mellow and nutty with a long lasting bittersweet chocolate finish.

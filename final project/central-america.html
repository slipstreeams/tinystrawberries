Central America

Coffees from Central America tend to be smooth riders, well-balanced coffees with caramelized sugar and honey notes overlying a mellow fruit background, and sometimes carrying some spice notes. Profile Growing Conditions: Altitude: 1150 - 2050 m Mexico coffee growing altitudes: 500 - 1750 m Common Varietals: Caturra, Catuai, Bourbon, Pacamara, Typica Guatemalan Varietals: Antigua, Atitlan, Arabigo, Coban, Huehuetenango, Maragogype.
